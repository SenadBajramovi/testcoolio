THE DEFAULT HPR ELECTRONIC DEVICE CONFIGURATION SETS THE ELECTRIC CHARGE
TO 2.62 KwH. IT ADJUSTS THE HSA TO 1. IT ASSUMES THAT THE STRAP IS PUT
ON THE INDIVIDUAL'S BICEP AREA. 

THE CUSTOM HPR ELECTRONIC DEVICE CONFIGURATION ASSUMES THAT THE DOCTOR
OR THE OPERATOR HAVE REASONABLE EXPERIENCE WORKING WITH HPR DEVICES. DO 
NOT USE UNLESS YOU MEET THIS CRITERIA. FOR INFORMATION SEE PACKAGE HANDBOOKS.
